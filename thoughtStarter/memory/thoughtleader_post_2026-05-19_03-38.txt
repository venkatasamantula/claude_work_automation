"Always stay cool" isn't a vibe — it's a performance strategy. 🧠 Under stress, cortisol floods the prefrontal cortex and literally switches your brain from deliberate, reasoned decision-making to reactive, habit-driven responses — the exact moment you need clear thinking most [1]. Leaders who stay emotionally regulated don't just feel calmer; their teams show 23% lower turnover and measurably higher engagement than those led by reactive managers [2]. 🧊 Composure isn't the absence of pressure — it's the skill that keeps your judgment sharp when everything around you is breaking. Build one regulation ritual — a breath, a pause, a ten-second reset — and protect it like the performance asset it is. 🎯
