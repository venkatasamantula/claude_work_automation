Ownership isn't a vibe — it's a behavior with a P&L. 🛠️ U.S. employee engagement just hit a 10-year low at 31%, yet 72% of employees mirror their leader's ownership behaviors, which means the gap isn't motivation, it's modeling [1]. New research shows psychological ownership only converts into performance when it triggers intrapreneurial action — spotting problems, proposing fixes, and shipping without permission [2]. 🚀 Renters wait for a ticket; owners write the brief, run the experiment, and tell you what they learned on Friday. Stop hiring for "drive" — design roles where the smallest unit of work has a clear owner, a real outcome, and the room to be wrong out loud.
